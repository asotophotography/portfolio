Favorite Works

These are five of my favorite photos this year, each with a short reflection.

“Late Light”
This photo was taken at sunset, using warm shadows and the rule of thirds. I love how the natural tones and lighting shape the mood.

“Stillness”
A minimalist portrait with natural lighting. It took multiple attempts to get the subject relaxed. I used subtle editing to enhance texture.

“Urban Frame”
Shot downtown with strong geometry. I used framing to isolate the subject, and contrast between natural light and hard shadows.

“Reflections”
One of my experiments with water surfaces and reflections. I tried to emphasize symmetry and abstract shapes.

“Motion Blur”
This was a nighttime handheld shot with longer shutter speed. I embraced the blur as a creative tool to show movement.
